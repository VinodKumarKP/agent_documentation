[Docusaurus](https://docusaurus.io/) is a **static-site generator**.

It builds a **single-page application** with fast client-side navigation, leveraging the power of **React** to make your site interactive.

It provides out-of-the-box **documentation features** but can be used to create **any kind of site** (personal website, product, blog, marketing landing pages, etc).
